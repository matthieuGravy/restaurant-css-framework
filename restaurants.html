Our Restaurants

We began as a small team with a long-term project: creating a restaurant that offers an ever-evolving cuisine. Our initial customers discovered Area-2, and their feedback, vision, and our passion propelled us to evolve significantly. We expanded our restaurants through franchising and established ourselves in the prestigious cities of Bruges and Ghent.

Our Areas

Our restaurants are called areas, spaces where we create a unique ambiance just for you, personalizing your desires and professionalizing your cravings.

The Founder

I am Matthieu Gravy, and my growth is a result of everyone's contribution.

I learned self-reliance in the security sector and management in the restauration industry. The restauration industry was a unique experience for me. I started on a scooter in a center that was not well-organized, lacking a winning mindset. I transformed that center, its teams, and its figures. In just two years, by improving collaboration and consistency in processes, a center that had been in the negative for seven years increased its performance by 12% and became an example. I finished with a role as a shift leader, wearing the manager's hat.

For me, the key to success is setting aside one's pride and learning from others. It's never too late to start; you just need to give yourself the means to do it.
